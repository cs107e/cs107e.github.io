__March 2024: We haven't made an inventory of what we currently have in our sensor stash, come by OH and we can look together to see what we have available.__

There are many sensors students have successfully used in previous quarters, below is a partial list to show the breadth and variety!  We have a small lending library of some common sensors and can loan you one to try out before ordering your own.

### Analog

The GPIO pins read only digital input, so an analog
input/device/sensor will require conversion. The user manual indicates there is a GPADC (general purpose ADC) on the Mango Pi,
but no one has explored it to find out more (yet)!

* ADCs (analog-to-digital converters)
    [MCP3008](https://www.digikey.com/product-detail/en/microchip-technology/MCP3008-I-P/MCP3008-I-P-ND/319422).
* Rotary potentiomers
* Sliding potentiometers
* Analog Hall Effect sensor (voltage proportional to magnetic field)
