### Goals

Lab this week will be a working meeting with your team. You are nearing the halfway point. We hope you have made a good headway so far and see a clear path from here to finish line.

* How has teamwork been so far?

* How did you do on meeting your week 1 goals?  How do you feel about your path from here to completion?[^1]
* Start planning how to best show off your creation during the project demos.
* What can we do to help you finish strong?
